ARTICLES CONTAIN WELL-RESEARCHED INFORMATION AND CAN BE FOUND ONLINE ON WEBSITES, MAGAZINES, OR BLOGS. AN ARTICLE MAY BE ONE OF THE FIRST THINGS YOU THINK ABOUT WHEN CONSIDERING CONTENT WRITING EXAMPLES.WHILE SOMETIMES SIMILAR, THEY ARE OFTEN LONGER AND MORE FORMAL THAN BLOG POSTS WHICH ARE TYPICALLY MORE CASUAL AND CONVERSATIONAL (SEE MORE INFO ON BLOG POSTS BELOW).THAT SAID, BECAUSE BLOG POSTS OFTEN INCLUDE RESEARCH, THE LINES BETWEEN THESE TWO TYPES OF WRITING MAY SOMETIMES BE DIFFICULT TO DISTINGUISH.
